Watermelon Feta Salad

Description

This is a chunky watermelon feta salad with arugula and tomatoes is enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • Â½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugula
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Direction

    Step 1

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and salt together in a large mixing bowl. Add tomatoes,
    arugula, and red onion; toss to coat. Gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese
    to serve.
